I'm a designer, developer and mum of two living in Sheffield, UK. I'm passionate about empowering others through design and technology. I love learning and I try to teach myself something new everyday.

I owe my success to the 100 Days of Code challenge which took me from no coding experience to freelance web designer/developer in 100 days. Learning UX design helped to round out my skills. I really enjoy working on products end to end, from design and ideation through to development.
