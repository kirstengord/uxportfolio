about me

All the good stuff.

I was born and raised in Texas. I moved to the UK after I graduated from university, where I majored in Anthropology, the study of people and cultures. I have always been fascinated by the way people think and how that changes across time and space. I genuinely love learning so I do a lot of reading and listening. I taught myself to code during the second UK lockdown. Soon after, I started learning about UX design and found my happy place. A mix of tech, human behavior, and creativity. I really love what I do.

You can read about why I learned to code in my blog post: You Can't Be What You Can't See.

Fun Facts:
◾ My dad taught me how to juggle when I was 5.
◾ When I was 20, I spent the summer in Rome working in a hostel.
◾ I moved back to Texas in 2015 for a few years. My husband and I owned a food truck called The London Express where we sold fish and chips.
