ARTIST PORTFOLIO

Logo and Website Redesign

OVERVIEW

Problem: Chuck's portfolio lacked a consistent design and was confusing to navigate.

Solution: I created a clean, minimalist design that allows the artwork to take center stage.

My Role

Responsible for creating a new logo, website redesign and updating site on Weebly

Timeline

1 week- March 2021

Tools Used

Figma, Weebly

BEFORE & AFTER

Before

The wood pattern on the homepage distracted the user from the artwork and the font and color of the text made it difficult to read.

After

I chose a monotone colour palette to show off the artwork and kept the design consistent throughout the website.

Before

Because all the portfolio images had been sized the same, many of the images were cut off. The images were not organized into catagories making it difficult for the user to find certain images and get a feel for the artist's different styles.

After

I changed the sizing of the images so that each image was fully visible. I also catagorized the images by genre making them easier to navigate.
