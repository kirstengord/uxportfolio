texicana

Texicana is a concept food truck app created for the Google UX Design course. I was tasked with creating a visual menu app. Research showed that the coronavirus pandemic has changed customer attitudes to queues so I went one step further and created a visual menu and ordering app that helps to maximise social time.

My Role

I was responsible for the entire UX design process: Research, Wireframes, Protoypes, Visual Design, Testing

Timeline

6 weeks - April | May 2021

the problem

The pandemic has drasticaly changed consumer behavior. We are all much more likely to shop online and order takeaways from our phones. How do we encourage people to get out and enjoy themselves again?

the goal

Create an app that allows people to feel safe to socialise by automating the ordering process and reducing the need to queue.

my process

I began by conducting interviews to confirm assumptions about consumer behavior changes. I used this information to create a persona which helped to keep the user front and center during the entire design process. I researched competitors who use ordering apps to find ideas and pain points before creating a user story, story board and user flow. Then I created paper and digital wireframes. After round 1 testing with the low fidelity prototype, I created mockups and a high fidelity prototype for a second round of testing.

persona

In order to better understand the user for this project, I create the persona of Georgina who became my travel companion on this design journey, helping me to better understand the project from her point of view and to create the best possible user experience.

meet georgina...

  • 19 years old
  • Art student at University of Sheffield
  • Lives in student accommodation with 3 friends, visits family a few times a month
  • Georgina is a talented painter and spends most of her time in the art studio. When she isn't busy panting she enjoys spending time with her friends. They all love to try out new restaurants and street food vendors. Georgina really struggled during lockdown and found it difficult be away from her family. She really appreciates the time she gets to spend with them now.

frustrations

  • Hates queues
  • Has difficulty seeing the menu when she is waiting
  • Hard to remember big group orders

goals

  • Enjoy time with friends & family
  • Try new things

"After being apart for so long, time with my friends and family is so important."

analyzing competitors

I looked at apps from two food halls in Sheffield, Cutlery Works and Kommune, to generate ideas for user flows and find pain points that I could address in my design.
