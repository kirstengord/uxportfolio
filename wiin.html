OVERVIEW

The Problem

The HR process is anitquated, inequitable and does not take into account the whole human.

The Solution

Create an app that disrupts the current HR process by removing the need for resume and cover letter writing. The app will humanize the process by providing advice and matching potential skillsets with learning and employment opportunities.
