work in industry now

Work In Industry Now is a startup based in San Antonio, TX currently participating in the 2021 Geekdom incubator. The Wiin app will simplify the recruitment process for companies providing apprenticeships and aid students in finding those opportunities. The app aims to humanize the process through onboarding and matching potential skillsets with experiential opportunities.
