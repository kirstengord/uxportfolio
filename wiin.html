work in industry now

Simplifying the recruitment process.

Work In Industry Now is a startup based in San Antonio, TX currently participating in the 2021 Geekdom incubator. The Wiin app aims to disrupt the current HR process by removing the need for paper resumes and cover letters. Wiin brings back the human factor through onboarding and matching potential skillsets with paid learning opportunities.
